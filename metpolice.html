As part of the campaign idea called “Your City” and the Metropolitan Police’s objective to hire more female Police Constables, I created a microsite which would look to hero the amazing women within the police force across all boroughs of London.

I worked with the pitch idea, “Your City” which looked giving women a sense of pride and duty for their city, London. This was done by showcasing the diverse and contrasting visuals that the city have to offer. The idea behind the microsite was, to demonstrate the longstanding history of women within the Metropolitan Police and how they’ve shaped the force today.

The microsite, coinciding with the campaign idea, was designed to feel personal to different users. Geo-IP detection of a user’s nearest borough, was used as a way of making the experience feel more personal with dedicated stories of current female Police Constables being assigned to each borough of London. A real-scenario quiz was designed to fit around user’s lives, with information such as their employment status, age and number of children determining the questions they would answer. The questions would be “what if” scenarios, asking the user how they would react to occurrences or events that happened with their everyday lives.

Outcome

The idea received positive response for its bold and unique approach to an institution such as the Metropolitan Police but did however unfortunately not go any further into development. The campaign idea and the digital product were perhaps a little too audacious in terms of the visuals for the Metropolitan Police
